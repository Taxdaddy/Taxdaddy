WE ARE MENUFACTURER AND MERCHANT EXPORTOR

GalaxyStone Pvt. Ltd. welcomes you to ancient, exotic and spiritual India. The company was established in 2019 and is a Export Base Indian company.

We have Extensive mining operation and produce more than 1 million sq.mt Marble and Granites and 100000 sq.mt of World Famous Makrana Marbles annually.

We are members of FIEO (Federation Of Indian Export Organisation), APEDA (Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority), and Spices Board Of India which are export promotion organizations and councils.

Our objective has been to develop our business as partners of our overseas buyers with emphasis on reliability as the key to our relationship.
